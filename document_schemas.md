# Document Types

## sleep_daily
Sleep score, duration, stages (deep/REM/light mins + %), efficiency, restfulness, HR, HRV, temperature, sleep debt, sleep inertia, toss/turn count, sleep cycles, nocturnal score, WASO

## sleep_weekly
Avg score, duration, stage breakdown, schedule consistency, trends, best/worst nights, cumulative sleep debt, avg restlessness, avg sleep cycles, avg nocturnal score

## sleep_monthly
Monthly sleep summary with aggregated metrics, long-term trends, sleep debt trend, restlessness patterns, sleep cycle patterns

## movement_daily
Movement score/state, steps, activity balance, training load, calories, active minutes

## movement_weekly
Total steps, workout count/details, activity trends, highlight days

## movement_monthly
Monthly step totals, workout frequency, long-term activity patterns

## cardiovascular_weekly
HRV trends (avg, baseline comparison), RHR (day/night), HR recovery, AFib screening

## cardiovascular_monthly
HRV/RHR trend analysis, heart rhythm screening history

## recovery_weekly
Body signals score, Restoration %, HRV/RHR contributors, cortisol rhythm, stress events, breath sessions

## recovery_monthly
Stress patterns, autonomic balance trends, recovery consistency

## fitness_weekly
Workouts (type, duration, HR zones, calories), VO2 max, HR recovery, training load, breath sessions

## fitness_monthly
Training load trends, workout frequency, cardiorespiratory fitness evolution, VO2 max trends

## sleep_respiratory_weekly
Snoring duration/%, coughing events, breathing regularity, sleep apnea indicators

## sleep_respiratory_monthly
Breathing quality patterns, anomaly detection

## metabolism_weekly
Average glucose, time in target range, glucose variability, meal responses (CGM users)

## metabolism_monthly
Glucose trend analysis, pattern insights

## temperature_weekly
Skin temperature deviations from baseline, illness detection, cycle tracking

## temperature_monthly
Baseline drift analysis, notable deviations

## menstrual_cycle
Current phase, cycle length, period dates, ovulation prediction, phase-specific patterns (HRV/RHR/temp by phase)

## social_jetlag
Jetlag minutes (weekday vs weekend sleep midpoint), classification, circadian alignment interpretation

## phase_alignment
Chronotype classification, optimal activity windows, sleep timing recommendations, movement timing optimization

## user_goals_daily
Daily goal progress by category (Sleep, Movement, CGM), achievement status, completion rate

## user_goals_weekly
Weekly goals summary, daily breakdown, goal-by-goal performance, adherence interpretation

## biological_ages
Cardio age, brain age, pulse age vs chronological age, trends over time

## lifestyle_weekly
Vitamin D exposure/absorption, screen time patterns, wellness activity completion

## lifestyle_monthly
Sun exposure trends, digital wellness patterns, activity consistency

## user_context
Physiological baselines (HRV, RHR, temperature), physical profile (height, weight, age, gender), sleep schedule preferences, menstrual phase baselines

## user_profile
Medical conditions, allergies, current medications, family history, lifestyle factors, health preferences

## blood_work
Lab results with biomarkers grouped by function:
- Thyroid: TSH, Free T3, Free T4, Anti-TPO, Anti-TG (affects: energy, hair, weight, mood, skin)
- Glucose: Fasting Glucose, Fasting Insulin, HbA1c, HOMA-IR (affects: energy, weight, brain fog, fatigue)
- Nutrients: Ferritin, B12, Vitamin D, Calcium, Magnesium, Iron, Folate, Zinc (affects: energy, hair, cognition, immunity)
- Lipids: Total Cholesterol, Triglycerides, LDL, HDL, VLDL, ApoB, ApoA1, Lp(a) (affects: cardiovascular health)
- Inflammation: hs-CRP, Homocysteine, ESR, Uric Acid (affects: fatigue, joint pain, heart risk, chronic conditions)
- Liver: Albumin, Globulin, Bilirubin, SGOT/AST, SGPT/ALT, GGT, ALP (affects: metabolism, detox, energy)
- Kidney: Creatinine, BUN, eGFR, Uric Acid, Sodium, Potassium, Chloride (affects: fluid balance, blood pressure)
- Hormones: Cortisol, Testosterone, Estradiol, SHBG, DHEA-S, FSH, LH, Prolactin (affects: energy, mood, libido, stress)
- CBC: Hemoglobin, Hematocrit, RBC, WBC, Platelets, MCV, MCH, MCHC, RDW, differentials (affects: oxygen delivery, immunity, fatigue)

## user_upload
Structured summaries from uploaded medical documents (labs, prescriptions, reports)

## data_inventory
What data types exist for the user, date ranges available

## product_knowledge
Ultrahuman metric explanations, score calculations, terminology definitions

## sleep_records
All-time sleep personal bests: best/worst scores, longest/shortest duration, best efficiency, best REM/deep nights

## movement_records
All-time movement bests: best/worst scores, highest step count, longest streaks

## vitals_records
All-time vitals bests: lowest RHR, best HRV, temperature records

## recovery_records
All-time recovery bests: best/worst recovery scores

## health_milestones
Major health achievements: best health week, cross-domain achievements, lifetime stats

## glp1_general
GLP-1 medication journey: medication type, dose history, metrics while on medication, safety flags, symptom history

## glp1_daily
Daily GLP-1 insight: today's metrics vs baseline, deviation analysis, post-dose recovery status
