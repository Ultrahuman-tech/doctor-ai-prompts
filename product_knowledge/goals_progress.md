# Goals and Progress

**Goal Types:**
- **Sleep Goals**: Sleep duration, bedtime consistency, sleep score targets
- **Movement Goals**: Step count, active minutes, workout frequency
- **CGM Goals**: Time in range, glucose targets

**Achievement Tracking:**
- Daily progress toward each goal
- Weekly completion rates
- Streaks for consistent achievement

**Goal Recommendations:**
Based on your baseline data, Ultrahuman suggests personalized goals that are challenging but achievable.
