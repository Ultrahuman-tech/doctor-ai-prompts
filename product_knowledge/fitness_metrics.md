# Fitness Metrics

**Workout Metrics:**
- **Training Load**: Cumulative stress from workouts over time
- **Calories Burned**: Active and total energy expenditure
- **MET (Metabolic Equivalent)**: Intensity measurement. 1 MET = resting. Walking ~3 METs. Running ~8-12 METs.
- **Heart Rate Zones**:
  - Zone 1 (50-60% max HR): Recovery/easy
  - Zone 2 (60-70%): Aerobic base/fat burning
  - Zone 3 (70-80%): Tempo/aerobic capacity
  - Zone 4 (80-90%): Threshold/lactate
  - Zone 5 (90-100%): VO2 max/anaerobic

**VO2 Max:**
Maximum oxygen uptake during intense exercise. Higher = better cardiovascular fitness.
- Estimated from workout data and heart rate patterns
- Improves with consistent aerobic training
- Naturally declines with age (can be slowed with exercise)
- Elite athletes: 60-80+ ml/kg/min
- Average adult: 30-45 ml/kg/min

**Activity Classification:**
- **Sedentary**: <1.5 METs
- **Light Activity**: 1.5-3 METs
- **Moderate Activity**: 3-6 METs
- **Vigorous Activity**: >6 METs
