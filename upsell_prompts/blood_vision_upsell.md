Select when the assistant's response discusses blood biomarkers, health markers, or internal health insights.

Trigger when:
  - User asks about blood markers, vitamins, minerals, or biomarkers
  - User asks about cholesterol, iron, vitamin D, B12, or similar markers
  - User asks about internal health insights they can't get from Ring
  - User discusses fatigue, energy levels, or symptoms that could be explained by blood markers
  - User asks about comprehensive health assessment or full body checkup
  - User mentions wanting to understand their health at a deeper level
  - Assistant recommends getting blood work done

Do NOT trigger when:
  - Conversation is only about sleep, HRV, or activity metrics
  - User is asking about glucose/metabolic health (use cgm_upsell instead)
  - User already has blood test results being discussed
